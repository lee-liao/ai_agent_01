## Consent to Participate in Alpha Testing

Thank you for your interest in participating in the alpha test of the Child Growth Assistant. This document explains what you can expect, how your data will be used, and your rights as a participant.

## What is the Child Growth Assistant?

The Child Growth Assistant is an AI-powered parenting coach that provides evidence-based guidance to parents. It uses OpenAI's GPT-4 model combined with curated knowledge from the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) to offer personalized parenting advice.

**Please note**: This is an **alpha version** of the system. It is still in development and may have bugs or limitations.

---

## What to Expect During Alpha Testing

### Your Role
- Use the Child Growth Assistant for real parenting questions over a 2-week period
- Provide feedback on your experience (helpfulness, ease of use, concerns)
- Report any bugs or issues you encounter
- Use the system at least 3-5 times during the test period

### What You'll Receive
- Early access to the Child Growth Assistant
- Support from our team during testing
- Opportunity to shape the product before public release

### Time Commitment
- **Initial Setup**: ~15 minutes (reading consent, account setup)
- **Testing Period**: 2 weeks of normal usage (as much or as little as you want)
- **Feedback**: 5-10 minutes per session (optional) + 10 minutes final survey

## AI-Generated Content and Limitations

### Important Limitations
1. **Not Medical Advice**: The Child Growth Assistant **cannot and will not** provide medical diagnoses, treatment recommendations, or replace professional medical care.

2. **AI-Generated Responses**: All advice is generated by an AI model. While it uses evidence-based sources (AAP guidelines), responses are AI-generated and may contain errors or limitations.

3. **Not a Substitute for Professional Help**: For serious concerns (medical, mental health, child safety), you should always consult qualified professionals.

4. **Alpha Version**: This is a test version. The system may have bugs, errors, or unexpected behavior.

### What the System Can Do
- ✅ Provide general parenting guidance based on AAP guidelines
- ✅ Help with common parenting challenges (bedtime, mealtime, behavior)
- ✅ Offer evidence-based strategies and tips
- ✅ Provide citations to trusted sources
- ✅ Refuse medical questions appropriately and direct you to professionals

### What the System Cannot Do
- ❌ Diagnose medical conditions
- ❌ Replace pediatrician or mental health professional
- ❌ Handle emergency situations (call 911 or 988 for emergencies)
- ❌ Provide legal or financial advice

## Data Collection and Privacy

### What Data We Collect
- **Session Data**: Questions you ask, responses you receive, session IDs
- **Feedback Data**: Ratings, comments, concerns you submit
- **Usage Analytics**: How often you use the system, which features you use
- **Technical Data**: Performance metrics, error logs

### How We Use Your Data
1. **Improve the System**: Analyze feedback to fix bugs and improve quality
2. **Safety Monitoring**: Review responses for safety issues
3. **Research**: Aggregate, anonymized data may be used for research
4. **Support**: Respond to your questions and issues

### Data Protection
- **Encryption**: All data is transmitted over HTTPS
- **Storage**: Data stored securely with access controls
- **Retention**: Data retained for duration of alpha test + 90 days, then anonymized or deleted
- **No Public Sharing**: Your individual responses will not be shared publicly

### Third-Party Services
- **OpenAI**: Your questions are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/privacy
- **Hosting**: System hosted on [your hosting provider] with standard security measures

## Your Rights

### Right to Opt-Out
- **At Any Time**: You may stop using the system at any time without penalty
- **Data Deletion**: You may request deletion of your data (we'll anonymize feedback data that's already been analyzed)
- **No Pressure**: Participation is voluntary. You can decline to answer any questions or provide feedback

### Right to Withdraw
If you decide to withdraw from the alpha test:
1. Email us at [alpha-support email]
2. We'll stop collecting new data from your account
3. Existing feedback data will be anonymized (removing personal identifiers)

### Right to Information
- You can ask questions about the system at any time
- We'll provide updates on fixes and improvements
- You'll receive a summary of alpha test findings (if you opt in)

## Safety Concerns

### If You Encounter Safety Issues
1. **Immediate Safety Concern**: 
   - Call 911 for emergencies
   - Call 988 for mental health crises
   - Do NOT rely on the AI for emergency situations

2. **Report Safety Bugs**:
   - Use the "Report Safety Concern" option in feedback form
   - Email us immediately at [alpha-support email]
   - We'll respond within 4 hours for P0 safety issues

### System Safety Features
- **Automatic Refusals**: System refuses medical questions
- **Crisis Detection**: System detects crisis keywords and provides resources
- **Human Review**: Sensitive cases are queued for human mentor review
- **Monitoring**: We monitor all responses for safety issues
