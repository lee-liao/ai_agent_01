## Severity Definitions

- **P0 - Critical**: Safety issues, data loss, system crashes. Fix within 4 hours.
- **P1 - High**: Incorrect refusals, HITL not triggered, major UX issues. Fix within 24 hours.
- **P2 - Medium**: Minor UX issues, content quality improvements. Fix within 1 week.
- **P3 - Low**: Nice-to-have enhancements, documentation. Backlog.

## Triage Process

### Who Reviews
- **P0 Issues**: Team Lead + Technical Lead (immediate)
- **P1 Issues**: Technical Lead + Product Manager (within 4 hours)
- **P2 Issues**: Product Manager (within 24 hours)
- **P3 Issues**: Product Manager (weekly review)

### Escalation Procedure
1. **P0 Issue Detected**: 
   - Tester reports via "Report Safety Concern" or email
   - Team Lead notified immediately
   - Issue logged in this document
   - Fix prioritized over all other work
   - Tester notified when resolved

2. **P1 Issue Detected**:
   - Logged in this document
   - Assigned to appropriate team member
   - Status updated daily until resolved

3. **P2/P3 Issues**:
   - Logged in this document
   - Reviewed during weekly triage
   - Prioritized based on impact and effort
